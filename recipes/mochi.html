Mochi

Description

Mochi is a bun-shaped Japanese dessert made from sweet glutinous rice flour, or mochigome. It has a soft, chewy texture that is somewhat elastic.

Mochi is often flavored with matcha (or green tea powder), which gives it a light green hue.

Ingredients

  • Red Bean Paste
  • Glutinous Rice Flour
  • Green Tea Powder
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
